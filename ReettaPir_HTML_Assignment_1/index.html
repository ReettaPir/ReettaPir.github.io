Introduction

Princess Mononoke follows a boy called Ashitaka on his journey to find a cure for his curse.
On the way, he stumbles upon a war between the people of Iron Town and the mysterious Princess Mononoke.

The movie discusses the coexistence and conflict of nature, technology, and humanity.





Main characters

  • Ashitaka

    A boy looking for a cure for a curse on his arm.


  • Princess Mononoke (San)

    A girl raised by wolves who is trying to protect the forest from distruction.


  • Lady Eboshi

    Ruler of Iron Town.
